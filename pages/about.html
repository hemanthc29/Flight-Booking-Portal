Baggage Allowance

  • Cabin Baggage: 1 piece up to 7 kg is permitted free of charge across all travel classes. Fits in overhead lockers or under seat.
  • Checked Baggage: Up to 23 kg check-in baggage is allowed for Economy passengers. Business & First class enjoy up to 32 kg baggage allowance.
  • Extra Baggage Surcharges: Additional weight bags can be pre-purchased during flight booking details at a flat rate of $45 per +15 kg bag.

Prohibited Items

The following items are strictly prohibited inside cabin and checked baggage:

  • Flammable liquids, lighter fuels
  • Explosives, fireworks, ammunition
  • Corrosives, bleach, mercury items
  • Lithium batteries (power banks must remain in cabin bag only)
